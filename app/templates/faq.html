Frequently Asked Questions

What AI models do you support?

We support multiple open-source AI models including DeepSeek Coder (for coding tasks), Llama.cpp (for documents and files), Vicuna (for images and videos), and GPT4All (for general chat). Our auto-select feature automatically chooses the best model for your task.

How does auto model selection work?

Our intelligent routing system analyzes your prompt to determine the content type. For coding questions, it uses DeepSeek Coder. For document processing, it uses Llama.cpp. For images/videos, it uses Vicuna. For general queries, it uses GPT4All.

What are the rate limits?

Free tier users get 10 messages per hour. Premium users get 100 messages per hour. Admin users have unlimited access. Rate limits reset every hour.

How do I upgrade to premium?

Visit our pricing page and select your preferred plan. We accept payments through Midtrans for secure transactions.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, CSRF protection, secure password hashing, and session management. We never share your data with third parties.

Can I use the API programmatically?

Yes! Premium users have access to our REST API. Check our API documentation for details on endpoints and authentication.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot password?" on the login page. You'll receive an email with a reset link. The link expires after 1 hour for security reasons.

Do you support multiple languages?

Our AI models can understand and respond in multiple languages. The platform interface currently supports English, with more languages coming soon.
